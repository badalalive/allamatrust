About us
Allama Rafiq Institute

President: Maqsood Ahmed

Allama Rafiq Trust was registered as a voluntry organization in 1996 under the Indian Trust Act. Founded on the premise that minorities should be provided with a level playing field, the Our Country does not need warm blood oozing out of our necks but it needs the sweat of our brow that would flow twelve months in a year. Allama Rafiq Trust has done significant work in the area of education, lielihood, advocacy and research on minorities issues. With advent of time, while still retaining its focus on minorities issues, the activitis of trust has become more mainstream and cater to the economically disadvantaged across all religious affiliations. The trust has been set up by a group of social workers who had a first hand knowledge of the issues of the minorities and the economically disadvantaged. The exposure to the field realities have over the years shaped the design of the initiatives run by the Trust. The work of the trust in primarily concentrated in the Walled City of Delhi where it runs a numbers of programmes related to the skill and vocational training. The Scholl managed by the trust in located at village, Himmatpur , in district : Hapur initially started for catering the need for liberal education for Muslim children now has on its roll children from all religious communities.
